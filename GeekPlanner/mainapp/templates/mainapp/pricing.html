Subscriptions

We offer different subscriptions for individual use and organizations. Community subscription does not have a trial period, you are eligible to use it as long as you wish. Ultimate and Enterprise subscriptions offer yearly billing.

Community

Free

  • Up to 10 projects
  • Up to 3 participants in one project
  • Up to 100 tasks per project
  • No additional features

Ultimate

$100 / year

  • Up to 100 projects
  • Up to 20 participants in one project
  • Up to 1000 tasks per project
  • Additional features

Enterprise

$250 / year

  • Unlimited projects
  • Unlimited participants in one project
  • Unlimited tasks per project
  • Additional features
